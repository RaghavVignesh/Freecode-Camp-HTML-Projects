Kadai Paneer

This is a recipe about making Kadai Paneer Recipe.Kadai Paneer is a spicy Paneer Recipe from North India which is served as a side to Roti,Naan,and Chapathi.The main Ingredients used are Paneer which is a type of cottage cheese from India and capsicum.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Capsicum
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions

Instructions

  1. Take Panner and Wash it
  2. Take Paneer Cubes and cut it
  3. Slice Onion and Slice Tomatoes into fine chops
  4. Add all Spices and Salt to the Panner,Onions,and Tomatoes Mixture
  5. Chop and Cut Capsicum into fine pieces
  6. Chop and Cut Capsicum into fine pieces
